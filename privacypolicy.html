Privacy and Policy

Fredun Group (“Fredun”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the confidentiality and security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information through our website (the “Website”). By accessing or using this Website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not access or use the Website.

Fredun reserves the right to amend or update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice.

1. Collection of Personal Information

Our Website is not designed to collect your personal information automatically. Fredun will only have access to your personal data if you voluntarily provide it—such as via contact forms, newsletter sign-ups, or feedback.

Passive Data Collection: We may use standard technologies like cookies, web beacons, and IP tracking to collect information about your visit (e.g., IP address, browser type, operating system, referring pages, and general location). This data is anonymous and used to improve site functionality, user experience, and analytics.

2. Intended Use of Personal Information

Any information you provide voluntarily will be used solely for the purposes stated at the time of collection, such as responding to queries, fulfilling service requests, or improving our offerings. All use will comply with applicable data protection laws.

3. Non-Disclosure of Information

Fredun does not sell or rent your personal data to any third party.

We may share personal data:

  • With trusted third parties under confidentiality agreements to support business activities (e.g., website hosting, analytics).
  • To comply with legal obligations or lawful requests from authorities.
  • With your explicit consent.

All such third parties are required to handle your data with the same level of protection as described in this Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse, or alteration of your personal data. While we strive to protect your information, transmission over the internet is never completely secure. Users should exercise caution when sharing personal data online.

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose it was collected for or to meet legal, regulatory, or contractual obligations.

5. Links to Other Websites

Our Website may contain links to external websites. Fredun is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such third-party sites. Users are encouraged to read the privacy policies of any linked websites they visit.

6. Cookies

Our Website may use cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us recognize repeat visitors, remember preferences, and improve performance. You can manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings.

Types of cookies used:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Help analyze usage and improve service.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.
  • Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver personalized content or ads.

If you disable cookies, some features of the Website may not function properly.

7. Children’s Privacy

Fredun does not knowingly collect or process personal data from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will promptly take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Object: Object to how we use your data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request a temporary halt on data use.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke your consent at any time.
  • Right to Data Portability: Transfer your data to another service provider.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact details provided below.
