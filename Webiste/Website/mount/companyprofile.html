Our Company Profile

Fredun Pharmaceuticals & Limited

Fredun Pharmaceuticals Limited

We aim to redefine wellness by making safe and effective medicines accessible across continents, empowering lives every day.

Fredun Granules

Specializing in effervescent granule formulations, Fredun Granules crafts innovative healthcare solutions for easier administration and faster absorption. Our R&D-backed process delivers customized granules for energy, hydration, and therapeutic use.

We are a trusted partner for many global brands in customized pharma packaging and B2B production.

Chuu Balm

A modern twist to traditional relief, Chuu Balm is our signature balm product designed for muscular relief, headaches, and nasal congestion. Its herbal formula is enriched with menthol, camphor, and essential oils.

Chuu Balm is known for its quick relief and is exported to over 15 countries worldwide.

Fredun Mobility

Focused on joint and muscular care, Fredun Mobility offers premium nutraceuticals, topical oils, and herbal blends to support active lifestyles. Especially beneficial for athletes and seniors, our products are clinically tested and internationally certified.

We believe mobility is freedom, and our mission is to help everyone move pain-free.

Freossi – Pet Care by Fredun

Freossi is our dedicated sub-brand for pets, offering high-quality medicines, nutritional supplements, shampoos, and wellness products for dogs and cats. Made with safe, vet-approved ingredients, Freossi ensures your furry companions live healthier and happier lives.

Our commitment to animal care is as strong as our dedication to human wellness.
