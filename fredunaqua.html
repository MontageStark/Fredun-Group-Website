BEST GUT

BEST GUT

  • BEST GUT
    • Composition:
      • Pediococcus acidilactici
      • Bacillus Licheniformis
      • Bacillus Subtilis
      • Bacillus coagulans
      • Paracoccus pantotrophus
      • Excipient (dextrose) q.s
    • Benefits:
      • The strength of the beneficial bacteria when used effectively reduces disease causing pathogenic bacteria and promotes beneficial bacteria of the gut region of the animals.
      • Improves digestion and promotes immunity.
      • Reduces stress and improves survival rate.
      • This is good for the general health of the animals.
    • Dosage:
      • Normal mixing ratio:
        • Mix Best Gut daily to the feed as shown below
        • Following doses can be administered for:
        • Cattle & Horses: 30 g – 50 g
        • Dogs & Cats: 5 g – 10 g
        • Sheep, Goats & Pigs: 10 g
        • To be given daily or as directed by the veterinarian

FREOSSITONE–F

FREOSSITONE–F

  • FREOSSITONE–F
    • Composition:
      • Biologically derived Microcrystalline Hydroxyapatite Complex (MCHC)
      • Equivalent to:
        • Calcium – 400 mg
        • Phosphorus – 190 mg
        • With trace minerals like Copper, Cobalt, Iron, Magnesium and Collageneous and Proteins
        • Fortified with:
        • Vitamin D3 – 1500 IU
        • Probiotics, Sea weed Extract, Spirulina
    • Indications:
      • Enhances moulting frequency in shrimps and prawns.
      • Minimizes the mortality due to moulting entrapment.
      • Controls loose shell and soft shell problems through bio-available calcium and phosphorus.
      • Prevents calcium deficiency syndromes. Optimizes absorption of bio available Calcium, phosphorus, magnesium and maximizes growth in a short time period.
      • Provides about 25% natural proteins, about 12% collagens and trace minerals.
      • It colonises animal gut, prevents pathogen growth and controls white faeces problems due to added probiotics.
      • Boosts immunity and helps fight free radicals due to the presence of Sea Weed Extract & Spirulina which also contains multivitamins & minerals.
    • Dosage:
      • Shrimps / Prawns: 5–10 gms per kg of Feed
      • Fish: 3–5 gms per kg of Feed
    • Presentation: 250 gm Jar Pack, 500 gm Jar Pack, 1 kg / 25 kg Laminated Bag
